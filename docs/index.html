Digital Minds — Organisation Directory

78 organisations active in AI consciousness, AI welfare, and digital minds research.

This directory maps the organisational actors in the digital minds field — the interdisciplinary space concerned with whether AI systems might be conscious, sentient, or moral patients, and what follows from that. It covers research centres, university groups, AI lab programmes, funding bodies, training programmes, and community organisations, organised into six research zones and four field-support categories.

Produced by PRISM — Partnership for Research Into Sentient Machines. Survey date: March 2026. PRISM is itself included in this directory.

About this map

The corpus was assembled through four systematic search rounds in March 2026, using seed sources (PRISM researcher network, EA Forum, 80,000 Hours, web search) and snowball sampling from included organisations. Each candidate was classified as included (explicit digital minds focus), adjacent (relevant but outside core boundary), or excluded (documented with rationale). Only included organisations appear in this directory.

Organisations are assigned to one of six topical zones based on primary research focus, or to a field-support category (funders, training, journals, community). Funding bodies must demonstrate confirmed grantmaking in digital minds research, not merely declared interest. Individual researchers without institutional anchors are excluded.

Limitations: search conducted primarily in English; non-English-language sources not systematically covered; private companies without public research output may exist below the visibility threshold. The full methodology protocol and decision log are available in the accompanying dataset.
